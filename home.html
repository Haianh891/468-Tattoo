ABOUT US

Love Machine Studio was originally founded in the Ukraine in 2011. In 2018, the studio was relocated to the center of Art Culture in New York City - Soho.

Our studio is not only for tattoos, it is an art project in which our creative team work in diverse mediums of art.

DESIGN

Our studio has the perfect balance of a clean contemporary design and inspiring street art that is unique to us. It is completely customized for the ultimate artist and client comfort.

Our artists are amongst the best in the business. They find our studio to be an inspiring place to be and are always pursuing ways to improve their craft through many different mediums of art.
